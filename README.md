## About

Earth Saving Solutions is a locally owned and operated licensed general contractor based in Denver, Colorado, founded by **Chris Hock** in 2008 with a vision "to be something more." What started as a small handyman operation has grown into a full-service construction, restoration, and design-build company with **20+ years of combined experience** across commercial, residential, and multi-family projects.

We do everything from fixing a broken pipe at 2 a.m. to ground-up commercial construction — complete with an in-house kitchen & bath design showroom. When an insurance company denies a claim, we take it to appraisal. When a veteran's home gets crushed by a tree, we show up with the whole crew and do it at no cost. That's the job.

> **Our focus:** Restoration with a strong construction background.

## Why Earth Saving Solutions

- **True General Contractor** — we manage the whole project (and the whole insurance claim) from the first phone call to final walkthrough. Not just roofers, not just remodelers.
- **Insurance claim advocates** — we've turned $4,000 denials into $48,000 approvals by taking claims to appraisal. If your claim is unfairly denied, call us before you call anyone else.
- **Eco-friendly by design** — construction is historically a heavy plastic-waste industry. We go out of our way to reduce our footprint.
- **24/7 emergency response** — fire, flood, or storm doesn't keep business hours. Neither do we.
- **Treat every customer like family** — a company philosophy, not a marketing line.
